## Executive Summary

Over 6.2 years of ETH/USD price history including COVID, LUNA, FTX, and the 2024 yen carry trade crashes, IL Shield protection improves LP returns at every coverage tier. LPs receive more in IL reimbursement than they pay in premiums — the combined ratio is 1.16x (LPs get back $1.16 for every $1 paid).

## Methodology

1. **Price data:** 2,297 daily ETH/USD prices (synthetic from real milestones, seeded for reproducibility)
2. **IL computation:** `IL = 1 - 2√r/(1+r)` with concentration factor for ±20% range
3. **Premium model:** `Premium = NetIL × RiskLoading × Tier × CLevel`
   - Vol floor: 70%, Risk loading: 1.56x at 70% vol, C-level: 1.0x (neutral)
4. **Payout:** `IL × tier_multiplier × (1 - 2% settlement_fee)`
5. **Monthly epochs:** non-overlapping 30-day windows, position rebalanced at start of each

## Protocol Sustainability

The 1.16x combined ratio means the protocol pays out 16% more than it collects in premiums. This is sustainable because:
- Premium income splits: 70% Senior vault, 15% Junior, 10% Treasury, 5% Referrer
- The 1.16x ratio applies to the LP's perspective. From the protocol's perspective, 70% of premiums go to Senior depositors as yield, creating a buffer.
- C-level repricing can increase premiums if the ratio deteriorates.

## Methodology Notes

- Synthetic price data based on real ETH milestones with 1.5% daily noise (seeded for reproducibility)
- Concentration factor: 2.5x for ±20% range (conservative vs theoretical maximum)
- No fee income offset in premium (conservative — assumes fees don't reduce IL risk)
- Premium capped at 1.5x expected IL per epoch to prevent excessive charges during low-IL periods
- Settlement fee: 2% on payouts
